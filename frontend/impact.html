ENVIRONMENTAL GOALS

Protecting Our Planet

Measuring our environmental impact through key sustainability metrics

75% Waste Reduction Target

Landfill Waste Reduction

By diverting plastic bottles from landfills, we aim to reduce waste accumulation by 75% in participating areas, extending landfill lifespan and reducing soil contamination.

60% Ocean Plastic Prevention

Marine Protection

Preventing plastic bottles from reaching rivers and oceans, contributing to cleaner waterways and protecting marine life from plastic pollution.

40% Carbon Footprint Cut

Carbon Emissions Reduction

Through efficient recycling processes and reduced need for new plastic production, we target a 40% reduction in carbon emissions per bottle recycled.

85% Material Recovery Rate

Resource Conservation

Maximizing material recovery from collected bottles, reducing the need for virgin plastic production and conserving natural resources.
