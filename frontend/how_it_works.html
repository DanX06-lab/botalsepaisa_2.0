FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about botalsepaisa

What types of bottles are accepted?

+

We accept all plastic bottles with recycling codes 1 (PET) and 2 (HDPE), including water bottles, soft drink bottles, juice containers, and cleaning product bottles.

How quickly do I receive my rewards?

+

Rewards are credited instantly to your botalsepaisa wallet upon bottle verification at our collection centers. You can withdraw to your bank account within 24 hours.

Is there a minimum withdrawal amount?

+

The minimum withdrawal amount is ₹50 for bank transfers and ₹25 for shopping vouchers. There are no maximum limits.

Are there any charges or fees?

+

botalsepaisa is completely free to use. There are no registration fees, transaction charges, or hidden costs.

Can I collect bottles from others?

+

Yes! You can collect bottles from family, friends, neighbors, or local businesses. Many users create small collection networks to maximize earnings.

What happens to the bottles after collection?

+

Collected bottles are sorted, cleaned, and sent to certified recycling facilities where they're processed into new products, reducing environmental impact.
