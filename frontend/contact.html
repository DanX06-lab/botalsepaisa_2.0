How can I become a partner?

+

We're actively seeking partnerships with beverage companies, recycling centers, and environmental organizations. Contact us with "Partnership Inquiry" to discuss collaboration opportunities.

+

Yes, we're currently in our funding phase. If you're an investor interested in sustainability and circular economy solutions, we'd love to discuss how you can be part of our mission.
