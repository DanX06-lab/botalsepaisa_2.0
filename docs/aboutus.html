COMING SOON

Transforming Waste Into Wealth

botalsepaisa is being developed as India's first comprehensive plastic bottle recycling rewards platform, designed to connect consumers with major beverage companies to create a sustainable circular economy while rewarding environmental responsibility.

Our Mission

To revolutionize plastic waste management in India by creating an accessible, rewarding recycling ecosystem that will incentivize environmental responsibility while providing tangible economic benefits to consumers.

Our Vision

To become India's leading sustainable technology platform, creating a plastic-free future where every citizen is empowered to contribute to environmental conservation while earning meaningful rewards.

OUR JOURNEY

The botalsepaisa Story

From concept to reality - building India's most innovative recycling platform

2024

The Concept

Recognizing the growing plastic pollution crisis in Indian cities, we envisioned a systematic approach to plastic bottle recycling that would benefit both consumers and the environment through innovative technology.

2025

Development & Planning

Currently developing strategic partnerships with major beverage companies including Pepsi, Coca-Cola, and local brands to create a comprehensive 60/40 revenue sharing model that benefits all stakeholders.

2025

Preparing for Launch

Finalizing platform development, establishing collection center networks across India, and preparing to serve users with zero operational fees while ensuring maximum environmental impact.
